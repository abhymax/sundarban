Things to Do in Sundarban

Beyond just the boat ride, Sundarban offers a variety of unique experiences.

Jungle Boat Safari

Cruise through the narrow creeks and canals. This is the primary activity to spot the Royal Bengal Tiger, Saltwater Crocodiles, and Spotted Deer.

Bird Watching

Visit the Bird Island (Pakhi Jungle). Spot Kingfishers, Egrets, Herons, and migratory birds. A paradise for photographers.

Dobanki Canopy Walk

Walk on a netted protective bridge 20 feet above the ground. It offers a panoramic view of the jungle and is thrilling yet safe.

Village Walk

Interact with the local villagers. See their mud houses, paddy fields, and learn how they coexist with the wild tigers.

Bonfire & Folk Dance

In the evenings (especially winter), enjoy a warm bonfire accompanied by the local 'Bonbibi Pala' or Jhumur tribal dance.

Mud Walk (Optional)

For the adventurous, a walk on the river banks during low tide to see crabs and mudskippers up close. (Requires special boots).
