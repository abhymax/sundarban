Sundarban 2 Nights 3 Days Tour

Overview: This is our most popular package. It allows sufficient time to explore the major watchtowers, enjoy the village life, and maximize your chances of spotting the Royal Bengal Tiger. This tour balances adventure with relaxation.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: pickup & Sunset Cruise

8:00 AM - 9:00 AM: Pick up from Kolkata (Indian Museum / Science City) or Canning Station depending on your booking.

12:00 PM: Arrival at Godhkhali Ferry Ghat. Transfer to our motorized boat. Welcome drink served.

1:30 PM: Lunch on the boat (Veg/Non-Veg). Cruising through Gomor, Durgaduani rivers.

4:00 PM: Visit the Bird Forest and enjoy a breathtaking sunset over the river.

6:00 PM: Reaching the resort/hotel at Pakhiralay/Dayapur. Evening tea and snacks.

9:00 PM: Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Core Jungle Safari

7:00 AM: Boat safari starts. We will enter the Sundarban Tiger Reserve. Breakfast served on board.

Sajnekhali Watch Tower: Visit the Mangrove Interpretation Centre and Turtle Bank.

Sudhanyakhali Watch Tower: This is the best place to spot tigers and deer.

Dobanki Watch Tower: Experience the famous Canopy Walk (approx. 500 meters above ground).

Lunch: Served on the boat while cruising through the dense creeks like Pirkhali, Gazikhali, and Choragaji.

Evening: Return to the hotel. Enjoy a local Jhumur Dance (Tribal Folk Dance) performance (optional/seasonal).

Day 3: Village Life & Departure

8:00 AM: Breakfast. Guided Village Walk to see the lifestyle of the locals (Honey collectors and fishermen).

10:00 AM: Visit Hamilton Bungalow and Beacon Bungalow.

12:00 PM: Lunch on the boat while returning to Godhkhali.

2:00 PM: De-board at Godhkhali and transfer to car/bus.

5:00 PM - 6:00 PM: Drop off at Kolkata.
