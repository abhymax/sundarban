Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the best time to visit Sundarbans?

The best time to visit is between September and March. The weather is pleasant during winters, making it ideal for boat safaris. However, we operate tours year-round.

2. Is it safe to visit Sundarbans?

Yes, it is completely safe. You will be on a motorized boat with experienced guides. The tourist areas are strictly monitored by the Forest Department. We strictly follow safety protocols.

3. What documents do I need to carry?

Indian nationals need to carry a valid Photo ID (Aadhar Card/Voter ID/Driving License). Foreign nationals must carry their original Passport and valid Visa/Permit.

4. What kind of food is provided?

We provide freshly cooked Bengali cuisine.
Breakfast: Luchi, Alur Dum, Sweets.
Lunch: Rice, Daal, Veggies, Fish Curry (Rui/Katla/Parshe), Prawns (on demand).
Dinner: Chicken/Mutton, Roti/Rice.

5. Is there mobile connectivity?

Network coverage is available in Godhkhali and most hotels (Jio/Airtel work best). However, deep inside the jungle during the safari, the signal might be weak or unavailable.

6. How do I book the tour?

You can call us at 097756 06350 or send us an email via our Contact page. We require an advance payment to confirm your booking.
