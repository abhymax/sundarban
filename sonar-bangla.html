Sundarban Sonar Bangla Luxury Package

Cost: ₹10,000/- Per Person

2 Nights | 3 Days - The Ultimate Luxury Experience

Experience the Sundarbans like royalty. The Sonar Bangla package is designed for those who seek adventure without compromising on comfort. From private luxury transfers to 4-star accommodation and gourmet Bengali cuisine, every detail is curated for your pleasure.

Package Highlights

  • Luxury Transfer: Private AC Innova/Crysta from Kolkata/Canning to Godhkhali.
  • Premium Stay: Accommodation in the best luxury resorts with river-view rooms and modern amenities (AC, Geyser, Wi-Fi).
  • Gourmet Dining: All meals included—featuring Mud Crab, Jumbo Prawns, Local Fish, and Mutton delicacies prepared by top chefs.
  • Exclusive Boat: Private luxury cruise boat with comfortable seating, clean western toilets, and sleeping cabins.
  • Entertainment: Exclusive tribal folk dance (Jhumur) and Bonfire evening at the resort.

Itinerary

Day 1: The Royal Welcome

8:00 AM: Pickup from Kolkata in a private AC car. Refreshments served on board.

11:30 AM: Arrival at Godhkhali. Welcome drink upon boarding our luxury boat.

1:00 PM: Grand Lunch buffet on the boat while cruising through the Gomor river.

4:00 PM: Sunset view at Bird Island. Check-in to Luxury Resort.

8:00 PM: Evening Gala Dinner with cultural program.

Day 2: Deep Jungle Safari

7:00 AM: Bed tea and premium breakfast.

8:30 AM: Full day jungle safari. Visit Sajnekhali Watch Tower, Sudhanyakhali, and Dobanki Canopy Walk.

1:00 PM: Special Lunch served on deck.

5:00 PM: Return to resort. Evening Bonfire with snacks (Chicken Pakora/Fish Fry).

Day 3: Village Walk & Return

8:00 AM: Breakfast and a guided luxury village walk to experience local culture.

11:00 AM: Check out and cruise back to Godhkhali.

1:00 PM: Farewell Lunch.

2:00 PM: Transfer back to Kolkata in private AC car.
