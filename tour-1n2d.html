Sundarban 1 Night 2 Days Tour

Perfect for a short weekend trip. Experience the core of the mangroves and return refreshed.

Day 1: Departure & Sunset

Pickup from Canning/Kolkata. Boat cruise starts from Godhkhali. Visit Hamilton Bungalow and Beacon Bungalow. Sunset cruise. Night stay at Hotel.

Day 2: Jungle Safari

Early morning boat safari. Visit Sajnekhali and Sudhanyakhali watch towers. Spot wildlife. Return to Godhkhali by evening.
