1 Night 2 Days Tour

Short & Sweet Escape to the Wild

Sundarban 1 Night 2 Days Tour

Overview: Perfect for a short weekend trip. Experience the core of the mangroves, spot wildlife, and return refreshed. This tour is designed for those with limited time who still want the full boat safari experience.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Departure & Sunset Cruise

8:00 AM: Pickup from Kolkata (Science City/Indian Museum) or Canning Station.

11:30 AM: Reach Godhkhali Ferry Ghat. Board our motorized boat and enjoy a welcome drink.

1:00 PM: Lunch on the boat (Veg/Non-Veg) while cruising through the Gumdi and Durgaduani rivers.

4:00 PM: Visit Hamilton Bungalow and Beacon Bungalow. Enjoy the sunset at Bird Island.

6:30 PM: Check-in at the Hotel/Resort.

9:00 PM: Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2: Jungle Safari & Return

7:30 AM: Morning boat safari starts. Enter the Sundarban Tiger Reserve.

Sajnekhali Watch Tower: Visit the Crocodile Pond, Turtle Bank, and Museum.

Sudhanyakhali Watch Tower: Cruise through Pirkhali and Gazikhali creeks. This area has the highest chance of tiger spotting.

1:00 PM: Lunch served on board.

2:00 PM: Return cruise to Godhkhali.

5:00 PM: Drop off at Kolkata.

Inclusions

  • Pick up and Drop from Kolkata/Canning.
  • All meals (Breakfast, Lunch, Evening Snacks, Dinner).
  • Accommodation in Hotel/Resort (Family/Couple rooms).
