About Sundarban Boat Safari

Who We Are

We are a team of locals and passionate travel enthusiasts dedicated to showing you the true beauty of the Sundarbans. Located in Chandipur, West Bengal, we have grown from a small boat operator to a full-service tour agency.

Our goal is to promote Eco-Tourism. We believe in sustainable travel that respects the fragile mangrove ecosystem while providing our guests with a safe, thrilling, and comfortable experience.

Why Choose Us?

Local Expertise

Our guides are born and raised in the Sundarbans. They know the tides, the tiger trails, and the stories of Bonbibi better than anyone.

Safety First

Our boats are well-maintained, equipped with life jackets, first aid, and government-approved safety licenses.

Hygienic Food

We serve fresh, hot, home-cooked Bengali cuisine on the boat. We maintain strict hygiene standards in our kitchens.

Our Mission

To provide world-class hospitality in the heart of nature, ensuring that every guest leaves with memories of a lifetime, while actively contributing to the conservation of the Sundarban Tiger Reserve.
